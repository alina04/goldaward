How to Help Yourself

If you ever feel as if your mental health is declining, you always ahve to help yourself first. Always know that there are people out there who want to help you. Looking for help is never a sign of weakness, but it is a sign of strength beacuse you found the confidence to help yourself first. Opening up to other will help you realize that there are a lot more people who are going through the same situation as you. You are never alone.



Anxiety or Panic Attacks

When someone is having an attack, you never want to leave them alone. You want to respect their personal space and verbally tell them that they are safe with you and that you will not leave their side. Tell them that the attack will not last long and that they will be okay soon. Afterwards, keep talking to them to take their min off of their fear and if needed, talk to a trusted adult.



Suicidal Thoughts

When someone feels as if they need to harm themselves, never leave their side. Stay with them until they are with a trusted adult or if they have proper help. Encourage them to open up to you or someone who can help them.
