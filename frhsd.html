Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a crisis counselor 24 hours a day, 7 days per week.



Family Crisis Intervention

Family Crisis Intervention Unit provides crisis intervention for families in Monmouth County on a 24 hour basis. 732-542-2444 day / 732-996-7645 night.



Suicide Prevention Hotline

1-800-273-8255 - The Lifeline provides 24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones, and best practices for professionals.



Monmouth Cares

Monmouth Cares helps youth and families with emotional, behavioral, substance use, developmental, and intellectual disability, physical health needs.



Youth Helpline: 2nd Floor
h5>

2NDFLOOR is a confidential and anonymous helpline for New Jersey's youth and young adults. We are here to help you find solutions to the problems that you face at home, at school or at play.



Monmouth County Mental Health Association
h5>

The Monmouth County MHA provides advocacy and services to families in Monmouth County and identifying gaps in the mental health system of care in collaboration with community, county, and state partners to close those gaps.



NJ Hope Line

1-855-654-6735. Need someone to talk to? We are here to help. Specialists are available for confidential telephone counseling and support 24 hours a day, 7 day per week.
